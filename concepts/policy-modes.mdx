# Policy Modes

## Why policy modes exist

Policy modes let you introduce governance **without breaking production**. Instead of flipping enforcement on for everything at once, you can:

- start by observing (REPORT_ONLY / MONITOR),
- measure violations and false positives,
- then gradually enforce (ENFORCE) only where youâ€™re confident.

<Callout type="info">
Mode is a first-class part of the v1.1.0 contract. It must appear in the returned <code>Decision</code> and in emitted <code>AuditEvent</code> records.
</Callout>

- **Use when:** you want real evaluation signals but do not want to block production.

- **Use when:** you are confident in policy correctness and impact.
